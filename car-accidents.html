Car Accident FAQ

Common Questions After A Crash

How long do I have to file a claim?

Deadlines vary by claim type and circumstances. Speak with an attorney as soon as possible to understand applicable deadlines.

What if I was partially at fault?

Fault issues can be complex. An attorney can help review the facts, evidence, and how fault may affect the claim.

Should I speak with the insurance company?

Insurance communication can affect a claim. Many people choose to speak with an attorney before giving detailed statements.

What documents should I keep?

Keep accident reports, photos, medical records, bills, repair estimates, insurance letters, and witness information.
