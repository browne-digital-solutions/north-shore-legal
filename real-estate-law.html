Frequently Asked Questions

Real Estate Law FAQ

Do I need an attorney for a closing?

Many buyers and sellers choose legal representation to help review contracts and protect their interests throughout the transaction.

What is title insurance?

Title insurance is designed to help protect against certain ownership and title-related issues affecting property rights.

What can delay a closing?

Financing issues, title concerns, inspections, appraisal challenges, and missing documentation can all affect closing timelines.

Can you review purchase agreements?

Yes. Contract review is often an important part of identifying obligations, risks, and important deadlines.
