FAQ

Common Estate Planning Questions

Do I need a will?

Many individuals benefit from having a will to clearly document their wishes and asset distribution plans.

What is a trust?

A trust is a legal arrangement used to manage and distribute assets according to specific instructions.

How often should I update my estate plan?

Many people review plans after major life events such as marriage, children, retirement, or significant financial changes.

Can estate plans help avoid probate?

Certain planning tools may help simplify or reduce aspects of the probate process depending on circumstances.
